This includes:

- The full, curated control-level mappings between ODSF controls and specific compliance frameworks (including but not limited to NIST CSF 2.0, CIS Controls v8, ISO 27001, ISO 27002, SOC 2, MITRE ATT&CK, SEC cyber disclosure governance, GDPR, HIPAA Security Rule, PCI DSS 4.0.1, NYDFS 23 NYCRR 500, NIS2, and DORA)

- Update workflows, version control logic, and change-tracking for mapping evolution
- APIs and programmatic interfaces to the mapping data

The complete curated Mapping Library is protected as a database under UK and EU database rights, and the non-public elements (including scoring logic, normalization rules, internal annotations, and update processes that are not disclosed publicly) are additionally protected as trade secrets.

The Mapping Library is available exclusively through the ODSF SaaS platform under separate platform license terms.
